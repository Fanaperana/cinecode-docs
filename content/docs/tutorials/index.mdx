Tutorials are **lessons**. They take you by the hand through a series of steps
to build something. Their job is to get you started and give you confidence —
not to cover every option (that's what the [reference](/docs/reference) is for).

Work through them in order. Each one ends with a real video that **you** rendered.

## Before you start

You'll need:

- **Rust** (stable) — install from [rustup.rs](https://rustup.rs)
- **ffmpeg** on your `PATH` — `brew install ffmpeg`, `apt install ffmpeg`, or a [download](https://ffmpeg.org/download.html)

That's it. CineCode bundles its fonts and needs nothing else.
